Our Mission:

We set out to apply deep data analysis mixed with Machine Learning on our Dataset.
We wanted to apply a machine learning model to help predict and recommend beers based on one's preference.


Given all the types of Beers, we also wanted to visualize what types of beers were popular and a distribution of what was available out there.


With Machine Learning as one of our tools, we wanted to get strong correlations between certain types of beers and if there could be a determining factor on prediction of a person's liking towards a different beer.
